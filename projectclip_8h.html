class  ProjectClip
 Represents a clip in the project (not timeline). It will be displayed as a bin item that can be dragged onto the timeline. A single bin clip can be inserted several times on the timeline, and the ProjectClip keeps track of all the ids of the corresponding ClipModel. Note that because of a limitation in melt and AvFilter, it is currently difficult to mix the audio of two producers that are cut from the same master producer (that produces small but noticeable clicking artifacts) To workaround this, we need to have a master clip for each instance of the audio clip in the timeline. This class is tracking them all. This track also holds a master clip for each clip where the timewarp producer has been applied. 更多...
