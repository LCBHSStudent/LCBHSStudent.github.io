Maps to the coordinates used in QImages with the 0 point at the top left corner.

  1. Scale from [-1,1] to [0,1] with y01 := (y+1)/2
  2. Invert (Orientation of the y axis changes) with y10 := 1-y01
  3. Scale from [1,0] to [height-1,0] with yy := (height-1) * y10 x does not need to be inverted.
