详细描述

The audio envelope is a simplified version of an audio track with frame resolution. One entry is calculated by the sum of the absolute values of all samples in the current frame.

See also: http://web.archive.org/web/20180626235917/http://bemasc.net/wordpress/2011/07/26/an-auto-aligner-for-pitivi/
